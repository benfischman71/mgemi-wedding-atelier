Why It Matters
A Mission, Not Just a Brand

Growing up in Italy, Maria Gangemi was captivated by artisans handcrafting shoes in small family workshops. M.Gemi was born to keep those workshops — and their livelihoods — alive for the next generation.

Where It's Made
Italy, Always

Every shoe is sourced and produced exclusively in Italy, using leathers from Leather Working Group-certified tanneries — a leather tradition stretching back to the 8th century BC.

How It's Made
Small Batch, Always

Produced in small batches by specialist artisan partners — healthier for our artisans' business and ours, reducing environmental impact while keeping luxury Italian craftsmanship approachable.
