Part One

How the Atelier works

The Wedding Atelier is a bespoke shoe design experience — a private session with the M.Gemi design team where the bride works one-on-one to create a completely custom pair of wedding shoes, handcrafted in Italy. Every detail is hers: the silhouette, heel height, leather, color, lining, and embellishments.

It begins with a beautifully packaged gift box and an atelier invitation. From there, she books her design session, works directly with our team to bring her vision to life, receives a fit kit to confirm sizing, and her finished shoes arrive in a signature M.Gemi box — ready for the most important walk of her life.

We recommend gifting the atelier experience at the engagement or bridal shower — ideally at least 10–14 weeks before the wedding date. This allows the bride ample time to dream, schedule her design session, try the fit kit, and receive her finished shoes well before the big day.

If the wedding is sooner than 10 weeks away, please reach out to our team directly and we will do everything we can to accommodate.

After your design session, we send you a fit kit — a set of plain white shoes in the exact silhouette you have chosen, across a range of sizes. These are not your finished shoes; they are a precise fitting tool so that when your bespoke pair is crafted in Italy, the fit is perfect from the very first wear.

You simply try them on, tell us which size feels right, and we pass that confirmation to our artisans in Italy before production begins.

Both options are available. In-person sessions take place at our New York atelier — a truly special experience where you can see and feel the leathers, explore samples, and work side-by-side with our design team.

Virtual sessions are equally thorough and personal, conducted via video call with curated leather and design materials sent to the bride in advance so she can touch and compare everything from home.

Part Two

Choosing your perfect wedding shoe

If you rarely wear high heels, we advise against anything over 80mm — you run the risk of not being able to last the full day comfortably. If you love the look of a towering stiletto but have concerns about endurance, consider designing a second pair of M.Gemi shoes for the reception — that is exactly what our Dance Party Edition is for.

For those who want height without sacrifice, a 70mm heel is an elegant and far more wearable sweet spot. And there are plenty of beautifully designed kitten heels and block heels that offer a little lift without compromising on comfort throughout the day.

Never wear your shoes for the very first time on your wedding day. Once your M.Gemi shoes arrive, wear them around the house with thin socks for 15-minute intervals to gently soften and shape the leather to your foot. Italian leather responds beautifully to this process — it will mold to you over time.

If you plan to dance through the night, our Dance Party Edition is designed precisely for this: a custom M.Gemi slipper in a silhouette you have also designed, ready to change into when the real celebration begins.

White or ivory is traditional and brings a wonderful sense of occasion to bridal footwear — but at the M.Gemi Atelier, the choice is entirely yours. Many brides prefer tonal contrasts: champagne, nude, or soft metallics that complement rather than match. If you love a pop of color, your shoes can even be your "something blue."

When considering your dress, think about visual balance too. A highly embellished gown often calls for a more refined, minimalist shoe so as not to compete. A simpler gown, on the other hand, is the perfect canvas for a more statement-making shoe — and that is exactly the kind of conversation we love having in your design session.

It makes the most sense to think about your wedding shoes after you have chosen your dress — but before your first fitting, so the dress can be tailored to exactly the right length. Leaving it to the last minute is the single most common bridal shoe regret.

With the M.Gemi Atelier, we recommend beginning the process at least 10–14 weeks before the wedding. The design session, fit kit, and crafting process all take time — and the earlier you begin, the more time you have to enjoy the experience rather than race through it.

Absolutely — and this is one of the great advantages of designing your own. Because your M.Gemi shoes are made to your exact specification, you can design something that works beautifully beyond the wedding day. A nude leather pump, a champagne slingback, or a classic pointed-toe flat in a jewel tone will carry effortlessly into your life after the wedding.

The Italian leather our artisans use only improves with age — it will soften, develop a beautiful patina, and become more personal to you with every wear.

Prevention is the first step — apply a leather protector spray before wearing your shoes for the first time, especially if any part of your day is outdoors. Grass and stone can be unforgiving on delicate leathers and satins.

After the wedding, store your shoes in their M.Gemi box, stuffed with tissue to hold their shape. For everyday care, a good leather conditioner applied every few months will keep the leather supple and beautiful for years. If you ever want to refresh the color or have any repairs done, reach out to our team and we will connect you with our Italian partners.
